Leave distribution

19 weeks, explained simply

6 mandatory

Uninterrupted, full-time and immediately after the birth.

11 voluntary

Continuous or split into weekly periods, until the baby turns 12 months old.

2 additional legal weeks

Voluntary, in weekly periods, taken together or separately, until the child turns 8.

This guide only includes the legally applicable weeks and does not include any additional company weeks beyond those provided by law.

Payments during leave

Who pays and how much will I receive?

This is one of the most common questions. Here is the simple explanation.

🏛️

During birth leave

During the suspension of the employment contract for birth and childcare leave, the benefit is paid by the Spanish Social Security Institute once the relevant application has been submitted.

📊

How much will I receive?

The benefit is calculated based on the regulatory base. This information will appear in the company certificate that People will provide so you can apply for the benefit.

💼

And the company?

The company maintains its employer social security contribution obligations. If your salary exceeds the maximum contribution base, People can review your case according to the applicable policy.

📅

When does the INSS pay?

The INSS usually makes payments at the end of the month. Any payment incident should be reviewed directly with the authority.

People tip: when you receive the company certificate, review it and use it to apply for the benefit with Social Security.
Step by step

What to do and when

1

Before the birth

If you already know approximate dates, you can speak with People to organise the leave and resolve any questions.

2

When the baby is born

Send People any hospital document certifying the birth so the process can start. It is important that the birth date is correct to avoid delays in processing the leave.

3

Leave request

Send the completed and signed request form with the dates you want to take.

4

Company certificate

People will process and send the company certificate so you can apply for the benefit with Social Security.

5

Remaining weeks

To take later weeks, give at least 15 days’ notice and send a new request with the chosen dates.

Lactancia

You also have breastfeeding leave

It can be taken immediately after one of the parts of the leave ends. It can be taken as 15 working days in one block or daily: 1 hour per day, or two half-hour periods at the start and end of the working day, until the baby turns 9 months old.

During breastfeeding leave, the INSS does not intervene: it is paid by the company at 100% of the gross remuneration that would apply if you were working normally.

🍼
Vacaciones

What happens to my holidays?

If birth, childcare or breastfeeding leave overlaps with pending holidays, you can coordinate them with your manager and People to take them when you return, even if the calendar year has already ended.

FAQ

Quick questions

Are the first 6 weeks mandatory?

Yes. They must be taken immediately after the birth, uninterrupted and full-time.

Can I split the voluntary weeks?

Yes, the 11 voluntary weeks can be taken continuously or split into weekly periods until the child turns 12 months old.

How much notice do I need to give?

Each weekly period or accumulation of periods must be communicated to the company with at least 15 days’ notice.

Who pays me during birth leave?

During the contract suspension, the benefit is paid by the INSS. People will provide the company certificate so you can apply for it.

And during breastfeeding leave?

During breastfeeding leave, the INSS does not intervene. It is paid by the company.

Can I take it part-time?

Part-time use requires agreement between the company and the employee.

What about the 2 additional legal weeks?

They can be taken in weekly periods, together or separately, until the child turns 8.

What if I want to organise it with People?

Write to us and we will arrange a call to review dates, documentation and specific questions.

Questions? People is here for you 💙

If you want to organise dates, confirm documentation or resolve a special case, write to us and we will review it with you.
